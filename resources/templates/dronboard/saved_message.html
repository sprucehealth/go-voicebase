Treatment Message Template

All Spruce treatment plans are accompanied by a personal note to the patient, from the physician. The purpose of this note is to very briefly summarize the patient's diagnosis and introduce the plan itself. To make creation of this note more efficient, we've enabled physicians to save a stock template to use as a starting point. (It will still need to be slightly modified on a case-by-case basis, to include information relevant to the patient's particular diagnosis.) We've included a sample personal note below. Please either tweak the existing content, or craft a new one that you believe better reflects your unique voice.
