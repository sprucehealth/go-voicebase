With Spruce, your child's acne can be professionally treated by a board-certified dermatologist right from their phone for only $40.

HOW IT WORKS

A Secure Online Visit

Your child answers questions, provides their medical history, and takes photos of their acne in our HIPAA-compliant app.

Just like an in-person doctor’s office, your child needs parental consent to submit their visit for review by a board-certified dermatologist.

Easy-To-Follow Treatment Plan

After your child submits their visit, a board-certified dermatologist will review their case and provide a diagnosis and personalized treatment plan within 24 hours.

Their treatment plan will include any appropriate prescriptions, detailed instructions on how to use them, and a set of skin care guides that your child can access anytime.

Comprehensive, Ongoing Care

Any medications your child is prescribed will be sent digitally to their chosen pharmacy, available for you to pick up.

Should your child have any questions about their diagnosis, treatment plan, or prescriptions after their visit, they can message both a Spruce care coordinator and their doctor at any time.

MEET THE DOCTORS

Each doctor on Spruce is a board-certified dermatologist and has undergone a rigorous interview process, including full criminal and clinical background checks.

All doctors in the network also have in-person practices, so you can expect the same level of clinical expertise that you’d experience in a typical office visit (or better, if you would’ve otherwise seen a general practitioner).

WHAT IT COSTS

The cost of a visit on Spruce is less than or equal to the out-of-pocket cost of seeing a dermatologist in person on most insurance plans.

WHAT'S INCLUDED

A visit on Spruce includes:

A response within 24 hours from a board-certified dermatologist

A diagnosis and personalized treatment plan

30 days of post-visit messaging with your care team

WHAT HAPPENS NEXT

You provide consent for your child’s visit by creating a Spruce account and uploading a photo of your ID (so we can verify that you are of age to consent to the medical treatment of a minor).

Your child pays for and submits their visit in the Spruce app.

We’ll notify you via email when their case has been treated by a dermatologist and for any other major updates to their case.

You can sign in to your account anytime to view your child’s treatment plan and messages with their care team.
