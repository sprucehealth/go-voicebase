Who’s Here?

Here are some of the dermatologists already a part of this Slack community.
Dr. Beth McLellan
Dr. McLellan studied medicine at Wayne State University. She specializes in complex medical dermatology and oncodermatology at Montefiore Medical Center.
Dr. Lawrence Cheung
Dr. Cheung studied medicine at Columbia University. He runs a private practice in the San Francisco Bay Area, and specializes in eczema and psoriasis.
Dr. Maria Robinson
Dr. Robinson studied medicine at Pennsylvania State University. She specializes in dermatopathology and is a Clinical Assistant Professor of Dermatology at NYU.
Dr. Elan Newman
Dr. Newman studied medicine at the University of California, San Diego. He practices at the Sharp-Rees Stealy Medical Group, and is interested in general dermatology.
Dr. Jason Fung
Dr. Fung studied medicine at the University of Rochester. He runs a private practice in the San Francisco Bay Area, and is interested in general dermatology.
