Frequently Asked Questions

What’s Slack and how do I use it?

Slack is an easy-to-use, fun communication platform. It has a fully-featured web app, native Mac and Windows apps, and iOS and Android apps. Preferences, conversations, and what you’ve read are synced across devices. Push notifications on the go are helpful too.

Can anyone join?

Membership is limited to dermatologists. Also, because we’re committed to maintaining a high-trust, high-quality conversation, we ask that you either be nominated by an existing member or apply to join. Once you are approved, we’ll send you a link to create your profile.

How do second opinions work?

Please follow these guidelines when soliciting photo-based second opinions via Slack: no identifying features in the photos (tattoos, birthmarks, etc) and no full-face photographs (limit to just the area of the face absolutely necessary to show scale, location, and lesions).

Do I need to practice on Spruce?

No. Many of the physicians in this community also treat patients using Spruce Health’s teledermatology platform, but it's not a requirement. If you're interested in learning more about using Spruce Health for yourself or your practice, send us an email: md@sprucehealth.com.
